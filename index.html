Here is a timeline of Vincent van Gogh's Life:

  • 1853 - Vincent Willem van Gogh is born on the 30 of March, 1853 in Zundert, Netherlands.
  • 1866 - Van Gogh's parents send him to a middle school called Tilburg, where he is taught by an artist that introduces him to imprssionism
  • 1869 - Van Gogh's uncle gets him a position with art dealers Goupil and Cie in the Hague.
  • 1873 - Van Gogh is tranferred to Goupil's London office after completing his training, the time he spends in London is the happiest time of his life.
  • 1875 - Van Gogh is transferred to Paris where he becomes increasingly dissatisfied with how his profession commodifies art and is fired a year later.
  • 1876 - Van Gogh returns to England to work as a supply teacher at a small boarding school, during this time he becomes interested in religion.
  • 1877 - Van Gogh moves in with his uncle Johannes Stricker a theologian and prepares to take the entrance exams to attend the University of Amsterdam and study Theology.
  • 1879 - Van Gogh takes up a missionary post in a coal mining town in Belgium.
  • 1880 - Van Gogh lived in poverty in support of his congregation which was very poor. The priests took offence to his way of living and released him from his post. He starts drawing the countryside and the people around him, so his brother Theo suggests he start focusing on art.
  • 1881 - Van Gogh goes to the Hague to try to sell paintings and meet with a cousin who is a successful artist. His cousin suggests van Gogh start working in charcoal and pastels, which he does.
  • 1884 - Van Gogh focuses on drawing and painting, working outside, his neighbor, Margot Begemann joins him on his excursions. They wish to marry, but their parents forbid it.
  • 1885 - Van Gogh completes many still lifes in mostly dark, sombre colors, he has his first public art show.
  • 1886 - Van Gogh moves to Antwerp, he starts studying color theory, and introducing bright colors into his work. He attends the Academie of Fine Arts, but has many conflicts with his teachers over his style.
  • 1887 - Van Gogh moves to Paris to live with his brother Theo. He becomes interested in Japanese ukiyo-e woodblock prints and is influenced by them in his work. WHile in Paris, he meets Paul Gauguin and they exchange work.
  • 1888 - Van Gogh moves to Aries, during this time he paints many of his most famous works.
  • 1889 - After experiencing acute mania, van Gogh starts including swirling lines within his work, during this time he completes Starry Night.
  • 1890 - Van Gogh dies on 29th of July, 1890 at the age of 37 in Auvers-sur-Oise, France of a self-inflicted gunshot wound.
"For my part I know nothing with any certainty, but the sight of the stars makes me dream." - Vincent van Gogh
